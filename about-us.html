Our Story

Founded in 2017, Angkor Coffee and Bakery opened the first coffee shop in one small house near Angkor Toch Temple. Later, the franchise was spreaded over Cambodia. All that we do is about you. From culinary specialists who make energizing new flavors, to group individuals who know precisely how you need your beverage—we organize what you have to get you on your way. We endeavor to keep you taking care of business, and we stay faithful to you, your preferences and your time. That is the thing that America keeps running on.

Harry Price
